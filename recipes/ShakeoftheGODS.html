Shake of the GODS

Bulk, like never before

Simple, easy and convenient way to pack in the macros

It's like Reece's Pieces for the aesthetically inclined!

Ingredients

  • 60g rolled oats
  • 30g peanut butter (smooth preferred)
  • 1 medium banana
  • 40g whey protein powder
  • 300ml milk
  • 5g creatine (optional)

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend to oblivion
  3. CONSUME
  4. Profit?
