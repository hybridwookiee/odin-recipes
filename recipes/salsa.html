Homemade Salsa

Easy homemade Salsa dip

Light, zesty and refreshing

The PERFECT addition to any taco night!

Ingredients

  • 6 Roma tomatoes (ripe)
  • 400g diced tomatoes
  • 2 spring onions
  • 1/3 cup chopped red onion
  • 1 jalapeno pepper
  • 1/3 cup fresh coriander
  • 1 large clove garlic, roughly chopped
  • 2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp granulated sugar

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients into a food processor
  2. Blend till slightly chunky (or to preference)
  3. Serve with corn chips/tortilla chips or on any dish as a sauce!
  4. (Can be stored in fridge for up to a week)
