Mac and Cheese

Quick, Easy and TASTY

Crockpot Mac and Cheese with creamy sauce.

Simple slow cooker meal great for a large family gathering!

Ingredients

    1 450g package elbow macaroni 1/2 cup of butter Salt and pepper to taste 1 450g package shredded Cheddar cheese 2 eggs, well beaten 2 cups whole milk 1 300g can condensed Cheddar cheese soup (Campbells) 1 pinch paprika, or as desired
